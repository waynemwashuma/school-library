The Disappearing Spoon

Author: Sam Kean

Category: Non‑fiction

Published: 2010

Status: Available

Description

The Disappearing Spoon tells the fascinating stories behind the elements of the periodic table, from their discovery to their dramatic impacts on history, science and human lives. The book weaves together quirky anecdotes, vivid historical accounts and unexpected science: gallium spoons that melt in hot tea; the bizarre fates of element discoverers; poison, war and scientific rivalries; and how each element has shaped everything from medicine to warfare.

Easy to read for non‑scientists, yet rich in detail and human drama, The Disappearing Spoon makes chemistry, and the history of science, thrilling. It reveals how the periodic table is not just a chart on a wall, but a cast of characters whose stories span love, obsession, madness, triumph and tragedy.
