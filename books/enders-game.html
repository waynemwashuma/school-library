Ender's Game

Author: Orson Scott Card

Category: Science Fiction

Published: 1985

Status: Available

Description

Ender's Game is a gripping science‑fiction novel set in a future where humanity, recovering from two devastating wars with an alien species known as the Formics (or “buggers”), scrambles to prepare for a feared third invasion. To build the next generation of commanders, gifted children are recruited and trained in an elite orbiting Battle School. Among them is Andrew “Ender” Wiggin, a boy whose tactical genius and psychological resilience set him apart.

As Ender advances through increasingly difficult war games, including zero‑gravity battles and simulated fleet commands, he faces intense pressure, isolation and moral dilemmas. The novel explores themes of leadership, the ethics of war, innocence lost and what it means to be human in times of conflict.
