The Girl Who Could Read Hearts

Author: Sherry Maysonave

Category: Mystery

Published: 2016

Status: Available

Description

The Girl Who Could Read Hearts tells the story of Kate Kindrick, a young girl born with a rare “seventh sense,” the ability to actually read human hearts and perceive their inner colors, emotions and hidden truths. As Kate grows up, she struggles with skepticism, misunderstanding from family members and the challenge of distinguishing between ordinary reality and the mystical visions that come with her gift.

When tragedy strikes and Kate ignores inner warnings from her gift and her guardian angel doll, her life and that of her loved ones, spirals into danger. Throughout the turmoil, Kate learns about faith, intuition, the power of trust and how to navigate a world where what is seen isn’t always the whole truth. The novel blends spiritual themes, family drama and a journey of self‑acceptance, making it a touching story about inner gifts, hope and healing.
