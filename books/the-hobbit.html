The Hobbit

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Category: Science Fiction

Published: 1937

Status: Available

Description

The Hobbit tells the story of Bilbo Baggins, a peace‑loving hobbit from the Shire, who is swept into a grand adventure when wizard Gandalf and a band of dwarves invite him to help reclaim the dwarves’ homeland and treasure from the fearsome dragon Smaug. Along his journey across Middle‑earth, through forests, mountains, goblin caves and dragon‑guarded halls, Bilbo encounters trolls, goblins, giant spiders, elves and other dangers.

Over the course of the quest, Bilbo transforms from a comfort‑loving homebody into a clever, courageous and resourceful adventurer. The book weaves together themes of bravery, friendship, personal growth and the clash between simple comfort and epic adventure, making The Hobbit a timeless classic of fantasy literature.
