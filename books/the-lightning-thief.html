Percy Jackson: The Lightning Thief

Author: Rick Riordan

Category: Science Fiction

Published: 2005

Status: Available

Description

Percy Jackson is a twelve‑year‑old boy who has been kicked out of multiple boarding schools and lately strange things have been happening: his teacher transforms into a monster, mythical creatures seem to appear out of nowhere and the ancient Greek gods might be real after all.

When Percy discovers he is a demigod, the son of the sea god Poseidon, he is whisked away to a secret camp for kids like him. Soon, he learns that Zeus’s master lightning bolt has been stolen and Percy is the prime suspect. Together with his friends, he embarks on a dangerous quest across the United States to recover the bolt, confront monsters, gods and betrayal and prevent a war among the Olympians.
