The Amber Spyglass

Author: Philip Pullman

Category: Fantasy / Young adult

Published: 2000

Status: Available

Description

The Amber Spyglass is the third and final novel in the His Dark Materials trilogy. As parallel worlds collide, protagonists Lyra Silvertongue and Will Parry, along with allies like Mary Malone and Iorek Byrnison, undertake a perilous final journey. They must confront cosmic forces, explore strange universes (including one inhabited by mystical creatures called Mulefa) and make profound sacrifices to challenge the power of the oppressive Magisterium and unveil the true nature of “Dust.”

Blending philosophy, adventure and heartbreak, The Amber Spyglass delivers a sweeping conclusion, weaving together love, loss, freedom and the enduring power of conscience as Lyra and Will face the ultimate stakes: the fate of consciousness, humanity and multiple worlds.
