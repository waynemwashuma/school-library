Library Rules

1. General Conduct

  • Maintain silence inside the library; speak quietly when necessary.
  • Keep mobile phones on silent mode and avoid calls in reading areas.
  • No food, sealed drinks in reusable containers only at designated areas.
  • Respect library property and other users
  • handle furniture and books with care.

2. Borrowing Books

  • Students may borrow up to 2 books at a time.
  • Standard loan period is 14 days. Shorter periods may apply to new or reference titles.
  • Present your student ID or library card when borrowing.
  • Reservations: reserve books at the help desk; pick-up within 3 days.

3. Returning Books

  • Return books on or before the due date to avoid penalties.
  • Late returns may result in temporary suspension of borrowing privileges.
  • Damaged or lost books must be reported immediately and replaced or paid for.
  • Use the designated return desk or book drop box.

4. Using Library Materials

  • Handle all materials carefully, do not write in, fold, or tear pages.
  • Reference books, magazines and newspapers must be used within the library.
  • Photocopying/scanning is allowed following copyright rules and school policy.

5. Computer & Study Area Use

  • Computers are for schoolwork and research only; personal gaming is not permitted.
  • Do not change system settings or install software on library computers.
  • Keep study spaces tidy and return chairs to their place after use.

6. Library Cards / IDs

  • Always bring your library card or student ID.
  • Do not lend your card to others. Borrowing with another student's card is not allowed.
  • Report lost cards immediately to the library staff.

7. Additional Policies

  • Bags may be checked or left in lockers at staff discretion.
  • Library staff may inspect materials when exiting the library.
  • Follow any additional instructions from library staff.
